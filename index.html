Common questions

How do I add a word?

Tap the + Add Word button on the home screen, type the word, and tap Save. LexiPower automatically fetches the definition, pronunciation, CEFR level, and an example sentence.

How does the quiz schedule work?

Every word follows a spaced-repetition schedule: hard answers come back sooner, easy ones come back later. The Review tile on the home screen shows how many words are due today.

My words aren't syncing across devices

Make sure you're signed in with the same account on both devices. Go to Settings → Data → Refresh from server to force a full pull from the cloud.

How do I export my words?

Settings → Data → Export my words downloads a CSV of your full notebook — words, definitions, CEFR levels, notes, and review state.

How do I delete my account?

Settings → Account → Delete account. This permanently removes your account, every word in your notebook, and your learning progress. The action cannot be undone.
